## Overview

Start managing your docs by creating a project in Unseen.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to `https://unseen.io` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    Click the **New Project** button on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details">
    Enter a project name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    Add team members under **Project Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin documenting">
    Click **Add Page** to start your documentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
