---
title: Can't Log In to Unseen
description: Troubleshooting login issues on Unseen.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page reloads
- "Invalid credentials" error

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Click **Forgot Password** and follow the link in your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your credentials">
    Make sure your email and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear your browser cache">
    Sometimes, cache causes login problems. Try clearing it and logging in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
