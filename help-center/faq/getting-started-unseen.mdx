## Overview

To begin using Unseen, sign up and create your first project.

## Steps

1. Go to `https://unseen.io` and click Sign Up.
2. Enter your email and create a password.
3. Confirm your email address.
4. Log in to your Unseen dashboard.
5. Click "New Project" to start organizing documentation.

## Tip

<Callout kind="tip">
You can invite team members to collaborate from your project settings.
</Callout>
